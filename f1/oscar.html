Biography

Oscar Jack Piastri is an Australian racing driver currently competing in Formula One for McLaren. He won the 2019 Formula Renault Eurocup with R-ace GP, and won the 2020 FIA Formula 3 Championship and 2021 Formula 2 Championship with Prema Racing.
